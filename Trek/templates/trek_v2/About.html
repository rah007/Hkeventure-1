Who We Are?

Started by 3 Alumni of IIT Bombay, Hikeventure's mission is to provide a unique and safe experience to the adventurers out there while supporting the local community, who make your experience safe and comfortable. We decided to start this journey after learning about the challanges local guides and people face and do not get their fair share from many vendors despite all their hard work. Let's explore the wilderness together and immerse into diverse cultures of incredible India.
